Male and Female Incomes from 1967 to 2017

The data used to create this chart is from United States Census Bureau, from 1967 to 2017. The male and female mean income data were adjusted by the Consumer Price Index (CPI) to match what it would be in 2017, so we can have a better idea about the wages gap.

Analysing the chart we can see the male income has been higher since 1967, starting with $38,957 and growing to $58,927 in 2017. On the other hand the female income starts with $15,978 in 1967 and goes up to $37,537 in 2017.
