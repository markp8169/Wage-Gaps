Is there a Gap Between Male and Female Wages?

Using 50 years of data from United States Census Bureau we can explore the wage gap between man and woman along these years and analyze it in order to predict what is going to happen in the future. Is the wages gap becoming higher or smaller?


On the links below you can find the charts showing the evolution on the female and male wages, charts showing different regions on the U.S., how the average income correlates with a person's education and race, a bar chart showing incomes per age range over the years and a prediction on what we believe both incomes could be in 10 years time.
