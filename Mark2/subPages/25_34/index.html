Gender Wage Gap

Gender Wage Gap for 25 to 34 years old Age Group

Correlations of Income, Gender and Age in the U.S.A. workforce.

This data shows the gender wage gaps from 1967-2017 using the average (mean) income levels in the U.S. and the gender quantity (in the thousands) in the workforce, broken up by age groups.

Data obtained from U.S.A. Census, table P-9 https://www.census.gov/data/tables/time-series/demo/income-poverty/historical-income-people.html
